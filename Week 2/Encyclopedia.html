What is a fern?

A fern is a popular vascular plant that can be found both domesticated and in the wild!

They are one of the oldest groups of plants that still exist today.

They have been able to survive and live so long because they are able to reproduce at a super fast rate

Each little leaf has a seed in it, so when it's windy or the leaves fall off, they are often replanted somewhere close by.

What a fern needs

  1. Moist soil with routine watering- they are native to more humid climates! However, they don't need a lot of water. Just moist soil
  2. Ferns love the sun! They love about 4 hours of sunlight a day
  3. When fertilizing your plants, probably once every 6 months, use a 20-10-20 to make sure appropriate nutrients are recieved.
